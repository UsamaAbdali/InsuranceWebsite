About Us

At Infinite Insurance, we have access to many Insurance Company products and services to better serve our valued clients. We put together customized Insurance and wealth building solutions for both individuals and businesses, as we strive to earn and maintain our client’s trust by helping them protect what truly matters most.

We specialize in 3 key areas:

Traditional Insurance

Life Insurance, Critical Illness Insurance, Disability Insurance, Long Term Care.

Guaranteed Issue / Non-Medical Insurance

Life and Critical Illness Insurance for individuals with previous medical conditions, who have experienced difficulties in qualifying for Traditional Insurance (Previously Declined or rated for Insurance).

Group Benefits

For companies with 2+ employees, offering comprehensive and competitive plans covering Health, dental and other benefits.

Other Products

  • RESP’s (Registered Education Savings Plan)
  • Segregated/Guaranteed Investment Funds (Registered & Non-registered)
  • TFSA (Tax-free Savings Account)
  • Travel Insurance
  • Visitors to Canada Insurance

Our Products

  • Term Life Insurance: 10, 20, and 30-Year Renewable and Convertible Term. Pick-A-Term also available.
  • Universal Life Insurance: Permanent Life Insurance offering flexible premiums, a level or increasing death benefit, and a wide range of tax-deferred investment options for individuals or corporations.
  • Whole Life Insurance: Permanent Life Insurance; both participating and non-participating programs.
  • Critical Illness Insurance: Provides a tax-free lump sum payment if you are diagnosed with one of 25 illnesses (such as heart attack, stroke, cancer.) Return of Premium options also available.
  • Disability Insurance: Protecting your income, in the event that you become disabled.
  • Long Term Care Insurance: Provides funds to pay for private Facility/Home care costs, while preserving your Independence and Estate).
  • RESP (Registered Education Savings Plan): Take advantage of the Gov’t 20% Grant plus the potential of an additional 15% bonus at maturity on all premiums.
  • Segregated Funds: Both Registered and Non-Registered plans available.
